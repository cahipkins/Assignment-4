DISNEY IN CALIFORNIA

TRIP TO DISNEYLAND IN CALIFORNIA

In 2019 we took our son to Disneyland in California for first time and had a great time and made so many memories such as:

  • My son first plane ride
  • Living in California
  • Going to Universal Studios
  • Visting Hollywood sign
  • Going to Disneyland

SON FIRST PLANE RIDE

At the time my son was 2 and had never been on a plane ride. We boarded at Philadelphia Airport and took a direct flight for 7 hours to California. My son did great. He was attentive and excited. He listened to music and watched movies until he feel asleep the rest of the ride.

CALIFORNIA LIVING

When we arrived in California we drove straight to our Air BNB in Anaheim, California and it was amazing. It was a mini mansion and it had a huge pool and hot tub which my son and I hopped right into within 20 mins of getting there.

UNIVERSAL STUDIOS

Our first visit outside was Universal Studios and my son loved it. They had all kinds of jurassic park and dinosaur interactive games and rides he could utilize and he loved it. The Jurassic Park ride was my favorite. We got on it twice even though the lines were long.

A DAY IN HOLLYWOOD

The next day we spend in the hills of Hollywood and downtown LA. We drove up the hills and took pictures at the Hollywood sign and walked around and looked at all the beautiful homes on the hills. We also went into downtown LA and walked the Hollywood blvd and ate great food at Santa Monica pier.

DISNEYLAND!!!!

DISNEYLAND WAS AMAZING!!!

We went to both parks in 2 days and we did so much:

  1. We had breakfast with mickey mouse in friends
  2. We got on roller coasters, trains, and played games
  3. We saw two live disney plays
  4. In the afternoons we watched parades that went through the whole park
  5. Lastly, they had fireworks. Both nights we watched the amazing fireworks show

My son loved Disney. He was so happy the whole time. It was a great experience and we plan on going to Disneyland in Florida for his birthday this year.

If you want to book your trip to Disney dont wait.
